projectYggdrasil

A Textploration Game

Welcome to Yggdrasil.

You will find yourself in a new world distant from what you know. Discover ways to explore and interact with the world. Colors will tell you whether you can interact with something and whether it is a Character, Object, Location, or Direction.

Start off your actions with a Verb. Somethings to try: Examine, Walk, Take.

It is important to remember there is no "win-state" or plot goals. projectYggdrasil is focused on Discovery and Exploration. Uncover rare Storylines, Locations, and Characters. Most "Progress" in this game is not linear. There is no expected route or right way to play.

And if you need help, just Ask ?
